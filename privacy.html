Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026
The short version: We collect only what we need to run your dashboard. We never sell your data. Your restaurant's financial data is yours — we just help you see it.

1. What We Collect

Account information: Your name, email address, restaurant name, and locations when you sign up.

Connected system data: When you connect your POS (Toast, Square, Clover), payroll system (Gusto, ADP), or delivery platforms (DoorDash, Uber Eats), we pull sales, labor, and payout data on your behalf. This includes transaction totals, labor hours, menu item sales, and delivery commissions.

CSV uploads: Data you manually upload to populate your dashboard.

Usage data: Basic analytics on how you use the dashboard (which pages you visit, how often you log in). We use this to improve the product.

Payment information: Billing is handled by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • Individual customer data from your restaurant (names, emails, phone numbers of your guests)
  • Employee personal information beyond what appears in payroll exports (hours, wages by role)
  • Any data beyond what is needed to populate your PrimeIQ dashboard

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and display your financial analytics dashboard
  • To send you daily digest emails with your key metrics (if enabled)
  • To send product updates and important account notices
  • To improve PrimeIQ using aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party. Ever.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (a US-based cloud database provider) with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to PrimeIQ systems only. We do not share raw data with any third party except as required to operate the service (e.g., Stripe for billing).

API credentials for your connected systems are stored encrypted and are only used to pull data on your behalf.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel, your data is retained for 30 days so you can request an export. After 30 days it is deleted from our systems. You can request deletion at any time by emailing justin@primeiq.io.

6. Third-Party Services

PrimeIQ integrates with the following services to operate:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Render — server infrastructure

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies. We have evaluated each for security and compliance.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data in your dashboard at any time.
  • Export: Request a CSV export of your data by emailing us.
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and data at any time.
  • Correction: Contact us if any of your account information is inaccurate.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies — only what is required for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you by email if we make material changes to this Privacy Policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: justin@primeiq.io

We respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
