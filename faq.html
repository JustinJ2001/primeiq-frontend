Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before signing up
Getting Started
How long does setup take? +
About 10 minutes. You enter your location details and fixed costs, then either connect your POS directly (Square works automatically) or upload a CSV export from your POS system. Your dashboard populates within a few minutes of uploading data.
What POS systems do you support? +
Square connects automatically. For Toast, Clover, Lightspeed, Revel, SpotOn, and any other system — you export your daily sales report as a CSV and upload it. We map the columns automatically. If your POS can export to CSV, PrimeIQ can read it.
Do I need to connect my payroll system? +
No — it's optional. If you don't connect payroll, your labor cost will show as estimated based on your target %. Connecting payroll (or uploading a labor CSV) gives you actual vs. scheduled hours and overtime alerts.
Can I add more locations later? +
Yes. Go to Settings → Locations and click "+ Add New Location". No additional cost — the $500/month covers unlimited locations.
Data & Updates
How often does my data update? +
For connected POS systems (Square), data syncs automatically every morning before 6am. For CSV uploads, data updates as soon as you upload. We recommend uploading a fresh CSV weekly or connecting your POS for fully automatic updates.
How far back can I see data? +
As far back as your CSV export goes. If you export 12 months of data from your POS, your dashboard will show 12 months of history. We recommend starting with at least 30-90 days for useful trend data.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored in Supabase on AWS infrastructure. Each account can only access its own data. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I export my data? +
Yes — use the Export CSV button in any dashboard view to download that data. You can also request a full data export by emailing justin@primeiq.io.
Pricing & Billing
What does $500/month include? +
Everything — unlimited locations, all dashboard features, daily and weekly email reports, CSV uploads, direct support from Justin. No per-location fees, no feature tiers, no hidden charges.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing or email justin@primeiq.io. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
Is there a free trial? +
We offer pilot access to a small number of operators before charging. Email justin@primeiq.io to ask about pilot status. The demo dashboard at primeiq.io/dashboard?demo=1 shows exactly what you'd get with your own data.
What happens after the pilot ends? +
You'll get an email from Justin before the pilot ends. If you want to continue, you subscribe at $500/month. If not, your account stays read-only for 30 days then your data is deleted.
Support
How do I get help? +
Reply to any email from justin@primeiq.io — I check it throughout the day. For urgent issues, email justin@primeiq.io directly. Response time is usually under 2 hours during business hours.
Do you help with setup? +
Yes. If you're having trouble getting your data in, email justin@primeiq.io and I'll walk you through it. Setup support is included in the subscription.
